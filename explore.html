FIELD OBJECTIVES FROM TWO FORKS TOWER

01. Trail Sweep

Inspect campsites, clear routes, and report any unusual activity along canyon paths.

02. Radio Check

Maintain contact with Delilah and log each call with time, weather, and coordinates.

03. Night Watch

Monitor distant smoke columns and keep visual notes as temperatures drop after sunset.

04. Supply Run

Retrieve provisions from the cache points and restock your station inventory.

05. Wildlife Survey

Document animal movements and report any signs of poaching or disturbance.

06. Weather Watch

Track storm systems and warn of approaching lightning or high winds.
