WEAPON DESIGNATION: AN-94 "ABAKAN"

The AN-94 assault rifle represents the pinnacle of Russian military engineering. Designed by Gennady Nikonov, this advanced weapon system features a unique two-round burst mechanism that delivers unparalleled accuracy and firepower.

Operational since 1994, the AN-94 has served elite Russian forces in various combat scenarios. Its innovative design allows for reduced recoil and improved target engagement at extended ranges.
