INTRODUCTION

A BRIEF OVERVIEW

Firewatch is a first-person mystery set in the Wyoming wilderness, where you play as Henry, a summer fire lookout. Through exploration and radio conversations with Delilah, the story blends isolation, suspense, and personal choices.

WHAT AWAITS IN THE WILDERNESS

PATROL THE FOREST

Explore the vast Wyoming wilderness as a fire lookout. Hike through dense forests, climb watchtowers, and discover the beauty and danger of nature in 1989.

RADIO COMMUNICATIONS

Stay connected with Delilah through your handheld radio. Your conversations form the emotional core of the story, revealing mysteries and building trust.

UNCOVER MYSTERIES

Investigate strange occurrences in the forest. Discover hidden locations, piece together clues, and unravel a web of secrets that span the wilderness.

STORY CHOICES

Your decisions shape the narrative. Every conversation and action influences your relationship with Delilah and the outcome of the mystery.

BEAUTIFUL ART DIRECTION

Immerse yourself in a stunning visual world. The award-winning art style brings the Wyoming landscape to life with breathtaking sunsets and atmospheric detail.

ORIGINAL SOUNDTRACK

Experience a moving musical score by composer Chris Remo. The ambient soundscapes enhance every moment of your journey through the wilderness.
